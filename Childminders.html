Your Questions Answered

Do I need to do anything special to join? ▼
No. You just need to be a registered childminder with a current DBS certificate and Ofsted registration. We verify these before your profile goes live.
How do I get paid? ▼
Families pay through the platform via card or bank transfer. You receive payment directly to your bank account within 3-5 working days. Subscriptions are billed monthly to your registered childminding business.
What if I'm already full? ▼
You can mark your availability as full or temporarily unavailable on your profile. You'll only receive enquiries when you're open to new clients.
Is there a contract or long-term commitment? ▼
No. You can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalty. You only pay commission on bookings you accept.
