Our Story

Why We Started KinCare Connect

As a Nigerian single mother navigating the UK childcare system, I faced a challenge that many African families experience: finding childcare that reflected my cultural values, language, and parenting philosophy.

I spent weeks scrolling through generic platforms that offered no way to filter by language, culture, or shared values. I interviewed dozens of childminders, many wonderful, but none who shared my background or understood the nuances of raising a child in the African diaspora.

That's when it became clear: there was a gap in the market, and more importantly, a gap in the community's access to culturally-aligned childcare. KinCare Connect exists to fill that gap.

Our Mission

To empower African diaspora families with culturally-matched, trustworthy childcare — and to help childminders build thriving businesses within their communities.
