Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe? ▼
Every childminder on KinCare Connect is registered with Ofsted and holds a current DBS certificate. We verify all credentials before they appear on the platform. You also get community reviews from other families.
How much does it cost? ▼
We offer a free tier where you can browse profiles. Our Standard subscription is £9.99/month, giving you full access to messaging and booking. Premium is £16.99/month for priority matching and priority support.
How do I know the childminder is the right fit? ▼
You can message providers before booking to ask questions about their experience, language skills, and approach. Many families arrange a trial session first. You also see reviews from other families in the community.
Can I use this for irregular/flexible hours? ▼
Yes. Our platform shows each provider's availability, so you can find someone who matches your shift work, weekend childcare, or flexible arrangements.
